WHAT IS OMNISCIENT?

It's a simple, lightweight app that makes your current active window appear as your Discord status, for example when you are drawing a masterpiece in Microsoft Paint or browsing the web on Google Chrome you can have it as a status on discord for everyone to see ðŸ˜‰

COMPATIBILITY

Every single app will show up as your status on discord, but only around 25 have icons that show up too in the status.
Currently compatible apps are listed on the Info section.
